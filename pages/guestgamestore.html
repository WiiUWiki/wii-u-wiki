Guest's Game Store is a homebrew appstore clone made for everything game related and to mimic the Wii U and 3DS eShop.

It currently includes Doom, Hello World, and a upcoming Omori chesto port.

It'll release early 2023.

It cannot interact with the Wii U eShop applet yet.
